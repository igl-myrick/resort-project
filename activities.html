At our resort we have an outdoor pool as well as a spa. Additionally, we provide surfing lessons, yoga lessons, and more. You can sign up for any lessons you may be interested in when you check in to the hotel.

Additionally, we work with the XYZ Tour Company to provide local tours of the area. You can contact them at the time of your reservation, or we will connect you with them upon your arrival.
